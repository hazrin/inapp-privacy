# Privacy Policy for PECS Learning App

Last Updated: June 2026

PECS Learning App ("the App") is committed to protecting the privacy of its users.

## Information We Collect

The App does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

## Local Storage

The App stores learning content and user-added images locally on the user's device using SQLite and device storage. This information remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to the developer.

## Photos and Media Access

The App may request permission to access photos or media files on the device so users can add their own images to customize communication cards.

Selected images are stored locally on the device and are not uploaded to any external server.

## Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect and process certain information automatically, including device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP addresses, and usage information, in accordance with Google's privacy practices.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy

The App is designed as an educational communication tool and may be used by children under parental or educator supervision.

The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Security

User-added images and application data are stored locally on the device. The developer does not have access to this information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
